JOIN THE CYCLE OWLS STUDENT CLUB!

We are a vibrant group of OU students who share a passion for cycling and adventure. Whether you are a seasoned cyclist or just starting out, our club welcomes you to join our community. We cycle regularly, at least once a week, exploring different types of roads and trails.

But that's not all! As a member of the Cycle Owls Student Club, you'll stay informed about all our exciting activities. From ride events and family outings to social gatherings and exam preparation sessions, there is always something happening.

Stay in the loop with the latest news and events by joining our email list on the Membership page. Don't miss out on the fun, camaraderie, and the chance to explore the great outdoors with fellow students. Come ride with us and make lasting memories!

Why Owls?

Because we share a passion for both science and cycling. Just like owls, we are curious, wise, and always ready to take flight on our bikes. Join us and discover the joy of blending scientific curiosity with the thrill of cycling adventures!
