ABOUT THE CYCLE OWLS STUDENT CLUB

The Cycle Owls Student Club is a vibrant community of students from OU who share a passion for cycling and adventure. We welcome cyclists of all levels, from beginners to seasoned riders. Our mission is to promote cycling as a fun, healthy, and environmentally friendly mode of transportation.

We organize weekly rides, exploring different routes and trails, and offer a variety of social events and gatherings. Our club also provides resources and support for students who want to improve their cycling skills and knowledge.

Join us to connect with fellow students, stay active, and enjoy the great outdoors. Whether you're looking for a casual ride or an intense workout, the Cycle Owls have something for everyone.

We believe in the power of cycling to bring people together, improve health and well-being, and make a positive impact on our environment. Come be a part of our community and experience the joy of cycling with us!

Our community thrives on sharing ideas and studying together. We organize group study sessions and discussions, providing a supportive environment where members can learn from each other and grow together.
